The Journey of Seva Bharathi Foundation

The journey of Seva Bharathi Foundation began with the vision of R. Gopichand Raju, a man driven by deep compassion and a desire to serve those in need. Witnessing the plight of elderly individuals left without support and orphaned children struggling for survival, he felt a divine calling to create a sanctuary where they could find peace, love, and security.

With the unwavering support of friends and like-minded individuals, he founded Seva Bharathi Foundation, laying the foundation of a mission rooted in selfless service and humanitarian values. His dedication has not only provided hundreds of lives with a new beginning but has also inspired a community to come forward and serve those in need.

His Long-Term Vision

  • ✔ Expanding the orphanage and old age home to serve more individuals.
  • ✔ Establishing a Goshala to protect and care for cows.
  • ✔ Restoring temples and promoting cultural awareness.
  • ✔ Creating employment opportunities through Ayurveda training programs for tribal communities.
