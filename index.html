About Us

About Us

Seva Bharathi Foundation is a compassionate initiative dedicated to providing shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support to orphans, elderly individuals, and marginalized communities. Our mission is to uplift lives by offering a safe and nurturing environment where the abandoned and underprivileged can find hope and dignity.

Founded by R. Gopichand Raju, with the support of like-minded individuals, Seva Bharathi Foundation strives to make a meaningful impact through its orphanage, old age home, educational programs, and spiritual initiatives.

Read More

Our Services

Orphanage

Providing shelter, education, and care for orphaned children.

We ensure a safe and nurturing environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive. Our programs focus on holistic development, including education, healthcare, and emotional support, to help them build a bright future.

Old Age Home

Accommodation, food, medical care, and emotional support for the elderly.

We provide a loving and respectful environment for seniors, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being. Our facilities include comfortable living spaces, nutritious meals, and access to healthcare services.

Education & Recreation

Programs designed for learning and well-being.

We offer educational and recreational activities to promote mental and physical health. Our programs include skill development, cultural events, and recreational activities to foster a sense of community and personal growth.

Spiritual Guidance

Bhagavad Gita teachings and moral development.

We provide spiritual guidance through teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and other sacred texts. Our programs aim to instill moral values, inner peace, and a sense of purpose in individuals.

Employment Support

Skill training and livelihood opportunities for seniors.

We offer skill development programs and employment support to help individuals, especially seniors, gain financial independence and lead dignified lives.

Temple Restoration

Promoting cleanliness and awareness initiatives.

We are committed to restoring and preserving temples, ensuring they remain clean and spiritually uplifting spaces for the community.

Ayurveda Programs

Training and employment for tribal communities.

We provide training in Ayurveda and create employment opportunities for tribal communities, empowering them with skills and knowledge for sustainable livelihoods.

Our Unique Strengths (USP)

Our unique strengths define our mission and impact: We provide a loving home, not just a shelter, ensuring education and growth for a brighter future. Our compassionate elderly care, holistic healing through Ayurveda, and dedication to temple restoration go beyond humanity. More than just an organization, we are a movement striving for a better world.

Long Term Goals

Our long-term initiatives focus on spiritual and cultural enrichment, livelihood support, temple restoration, cow welfare, and Ayurvedic awareness to create a sustainable and morally strong society.

Spiritual & Cultural Initiatives

Conducting Bhagavad Gita sessions, offering scholarships, and organizing competitions for school children to instill moral and cultural values.

Livelihood Support

Providing skill-based work opportunities for elderly individuals and marginalized communities to promote economic self-reliance.

Temple Restoration

Restoring temples and promoting cleanliness awareness in temple premises to preserve our rich cultural and spiritual heritage.

Goshala Initiatives

Establishing Goshala facilities to provide shelter and care for abandoned cows, ensuring their well-being and protection.

Ayurveda Awareness

Empowering tribal communities with Ayurveda training and creating awareness about its benefits among the general public to promote natural healing.
