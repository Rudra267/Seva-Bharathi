Our Founder: The Vision Behind Seva Bharathi Foundation

The journey of Seva Bharathi Foundation began with the vision of R. Gopichand Raju, a man driven by deep compassion and a desire to serve those in need. Witnessing the struggles of elderly individuals left without support and orphaned children fighting for survival, he felt a divine calling to create a sanctuary where they could find peace, love, and security.

With the unwavering support of friends and like-minded individuals, he established Seva Bharathi Foundation, laying the groundwork for a mission rooted in selfless service and humanitarian values. His dedication has not only transformed countless lives but has also inspired a movement—encouraging others to step forward and serve those in need.

🌿 His Long-Term Vision

  • Expanding the orphanage and old age home to provide care for more individuals.
  • Establishing a Gohsala to protect and nurture cows.
  • Restoring temples and promoting cultural awareness.
  • Creating employment opportunities through Ayurveda training programs for tribal communities.
